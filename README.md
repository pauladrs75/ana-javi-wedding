# Ana & Javi — Wedding Website 💍

**18 September 2026 · Guadalajara**

A bilingual (ES/EN) single-page wedding website with:
- Live countdown to the ceremony
- Day schedule (Ceremony + Cocktail & Dinner)
- Interactive Google Maps routes from Madrid (car & bus)
- Hotel recommendation
- Gift list with IBAN
- RSVP form

## Adding photos
Place your photos in this folder named:
- `photo1.jpg` — couple photo 1
- `photo2.jpg` — couple photo 2

They will appear automatically in the photo strip and gift list gallery.
